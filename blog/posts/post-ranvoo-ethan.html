What the AirJet X5 Actually Does — and How Well It Does It

The Sustainability Story — What Holds Up and What Doesn't

I want to be honest here because greenwashing is real and this community is good at detecting it. RANVOO makes strong claims, and most of them check out — but not all of them are as straightforward as the marketing implies.

The 47% reduced plastic figure is based on a comparison of total plastic mass across the handle, brush heads, and packaging over a projected five-year lifecycle. The methodology is published and the math is sound. The brush heads do use a noticeably slimmer collar than Sonicare or Oral-B heads, and the packaging is 100% recycled cardboard with soy-based inks — I checked. The handle itself is made from recycled ABS plastic, which isn't perfect but is meaningfully better than virgin plastic.

Where I'd push back slightly: RANVOO mentions TerraCycle compatibility on their site, but as of my research in early 2026, you're still coordinating that yourself — there's no prepaid return envelope in the box, no built-in take-back program. It's on you to find a drop-off or mail it in. That's not a dealbreaker for me, but if you're expecting a fully closed-loop system, manage your expectations. The sustainability story here is genuinely better than most, but it's not perfect.
