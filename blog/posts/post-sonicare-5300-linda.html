Why Gum Health Is a Non-Negotiable for Type 2 Diabetics

I didn't fully understand the gum-diabetes connection until my endocrinologist spelled it out. What that means practically is that brushing can't be a casual two-minute habit anymore. I need consistent, thorough plaque removal every single day.
