## Marketing Context

**Product Category:** Eco-Friendly / Sustainable Electric Toothbrush
**Assigned Product:** RANVOO AirJet X5 — $127.49

**Why This Look Works:**

Ethan's visual profile communicates **values-alignment** — every visual detail signals that his consumption choices are deliberate and principle-driven. The Patagonia fleece, the resoled boots, the Greenpeace sticker — these are not aspirational props, they are identity markers that the target buyer recognizes as their own.

The outdoor or near-garden setting grounds the product in its environmental context: this is a person who cares about what ends up in the ocean, and he has done the research to prove that this brush is better.

**Ad Copy Direction:** Lead with the lifecycle data, not the features. "47% less plastic per lifecycle. TerraCycle compatible. The brush you can own without the guilt." Avoid green-washing language — this persona detects it immediately. Cite the numbers.
