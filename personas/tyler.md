He is looking at his phone with the specific expression of someone watching a 45-second video that has just made them want to buy something. Or he is looking directly at the camera with the easy, relaxed confidence of a 22-year-old who does not overthink photographs.

**Lighting:** Warm, slightly yellow bathroom light. A string light visible in the doorframe behind him. Feels real and lived-in, not styled.

**Expression:** Casual, slightly goofy, at ease. Not performing — just existing.

**Camera:** Shot on iPhone aesthetic — 26mm equivalent, slight digital sharpness. Could be a TikTok screenshot or a candid. That is the point.
