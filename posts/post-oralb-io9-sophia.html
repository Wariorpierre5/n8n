The Invisalign Brushing Protocol

Your orthodontist's protocol typically calls for:

1. Remove aligners → brush immediately (before food, not after)

2. Clean aligners separately with aligner cleaning tablets

3. Brush teeth for full 2 minutes with electric brush, Orthodontic mode

4. Rinse and floss — flossing between appointments is critical for interproximal plaque

5. Replace aligners only after brushing is complete
